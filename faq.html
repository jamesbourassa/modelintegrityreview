What exactly does Model Integrity Review evaluate?
MIR evaluates the structural clarity, internal consistency, traceability, and documentation quality of analytical models and supporting materials. The focus is on whether a model is understandable and defensible as a decision artifact.
Do you validate or certify model correctness?
No. MIR does not validate technical correctness, certify compliance, or endorse conclusions.
