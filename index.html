Model Integrity Review

Independent, management-led review of analytical models and supporting documentation — focused on internal consistency, assumption clarity, traceability, and documentation quality.
Serving organizations nationwide. Engagements are document-based and conducted remotely.
Pre-decision Pre-submission Pre-audit Documentation defensibility
What we do
  • Structural integrity and internal consistency review
  • Assumption clarity and traceability checks
  • Unit / scaling coherence review (where applicable)
  • Documentation adequacy for audit, review, or executive use
This is a structural and documentation review of the materials provided — not technical validation or redesign.
What we do not do
  • Domain correctness validation or certification
  • Regulatory or legal compliance sign-off
  • Performance optimization, debugging, or model redesign
  • Endorsement of conclusions, predictions, or outcomes
Who this is for
  • Senior managers responsible for models they did not author
  • Consultants delivering model-based recommendations to clients
  • Research groups preparing work for submission or external review
  • Risk, compliance, governance, and QA functions seeking clarity and defensibility
How the service works
  • Inquiry & scope screening (to confirm fit and boundaries)
  • Engagement confirmation (scope, timeline, fee; engagement letter)
  • Materials intake (models & documentation logged)
  • Model mapping (inputs → assumptions → transformations → outputs)
  • Consistency checks (coherence, units/scaling where applicable, traceability)
  • Written review memorandum (typically 2–4 pages)
  • Delivery & invoicing (invoice issued Net 15)
Typical turnaround: 7–10 business days, depending on scope and materials complexity.
Deliverable
A concise written memorandum outlining scope, materials reviewed, observed strengths, documentation/traceability risks, and non-prescriptive recommendations — with clear limitations and disclaimers.
