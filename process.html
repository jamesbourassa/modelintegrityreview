Our Process

Home FAQ
  1. Inquiry & Scope Screening
    A brief email exchange confirms fit, scope boundaries, and expectations.
  2. Engagement Confirmation
    Scope, timeline, and fee are confirmed in writing before work begins.
  3. Materials Intake
    Client-provided documents are logged and form the sole basis of the review.
  4. Model Mapping
    Inputs, assumptions, transformations, and outputs are mapped at a structural level.
  5. Consistency & Traceability Review
    Internal coherence, documentation alignment, and traceability are assessed.
  6. Written Review Memorandum
    A concise memorandum (typically 2–4 pages) documents observations and non-prescriptive recommendations.
  7. Delivery & Invoicing
    The memorandum is delivered electronically; invoicing follows (Net 15).

Reviews are document-based, conducted remotely, and do not include validation, certification, or endorsement of outcomes.
